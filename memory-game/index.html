Instructions

Consentration, also known as Match Match, Memory, Pelmanism, Shinkel-suijaku, Pexeso or simply Pairs, is a card game in which all of the cards are laid face down on a surface and two cards are fliped face up over each run. The oject of the game is to turn over pairs of matching cards.
